Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

TL;DR: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information. All AI processing happens locally.

Our Philosophy

At Optamize, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. Our apps are designed from the ground up to respect your privacy by processing everything locally on your device.

Data Collection

We collect zero personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No crash reporting that includes personal data
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No data sharing with third parties

Local Processing

All Opta apps process data entirely on your device:

  • Opta MacOS: System telemetry and optimization happen locally. Your performance data never leaves your Mac.
  • Opta Scan: Document scanning and OCR use on-device AI. Your scanned documents are stored only on your device and iCloud (if you enable it).
  • Opta LM: Calendar and task data sync through your own accounts (Google, Apple, Todoist). We never see or store your data.

Third-Party Services

Some apps integrate with third-party services at your request:

  • iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through Apple's secure infrastructure.
  • Google Calendar: Direct API connection using OAuth. We never store your Google credentials.
  • Todoist: Direct API connection. Your tasks stay between you and Todoist.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure system APIs:

  • macOS: Sandboxed app container with Keychain for sensitive data
  • iOS: App sandbox with Keychain Services

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, you have complete control:

  • Delete the app to remove all data
  • Export your data anytime from within the app
  • Revoke third-party service access from your account settings

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out at privacy@optamize.biz
