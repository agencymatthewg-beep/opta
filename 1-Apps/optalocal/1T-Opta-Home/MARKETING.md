# Opta Home — Marketing & Capabilities

**Tagline:** Private AI infrastructure for Apple Silicon. Your compute, your control.

## 🎯 The Purpose
Opta Home (`optalocal.com`) is the definitive brand entry point for the entire Opta Local ecosystem. It acts as the routing hub that explains the philosophy of local-first AI, visualizes the architecture of the stack, and directs users to the correct management applications based on their intent.

## ✨ Core Marketing Points (What makes it impressive?)
*   **Technical Credibility First:** It rejects the generic "AI blob" aesthetic in favor of hard data. It features real benchmark numbers (e.g., `22.2 tok/s on 512GB Unified Memory`), architectural flow diagrams, and a definitive model compatibility grid (Qwen, Llama, DeepSeek).
*   **Ecosystem Bento Grid:** Visually separates the "Main Local Apps" (what you use daily) from the "Management Websites" (how you configure the stack), instantly orienting users who are used to chaotic, fragmented open-source repositories.
*   **Void-Native Design:** Showcases the premium "Opta Local" aesthetic characterized by `#09090b` void black, Electric Violet accents, JetBrains Mono data typography, and precision Framer Motion physics.
*   **Zero-Latency Static Edge:** Built as a pure Next.js 16 static export with no backend database, ensuring the brand hub loads instantly and is completely immune to server downtime.

## 🛠 Features & Capabilities
*   **Intelligent Routing:** Acts as the traffic controller, pushing new users to `init.optalocal.com` for installation, and returning users to `lmx.optalocal.com` or `accounts.optalocal.com`.
*   **Architecture Visualization:** Provides clear, side-by-side diagrams proving how local-active architectures are fundamentally more secure than cloud-proxied solutions.
*   **Terminal DNA Previews:** Features live, typewriter-animated terminal interactions that demonstrate the CLI's capabilities directly in the browser.
*   **Mobile-Responsive Precision:** Maintains the complex Bento grid and technical diagrams flawlessly across all device form factors.
