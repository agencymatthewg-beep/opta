Opta Ring Design Requirements

The Soul of the Application - A Living Artifact

Core Philosophy

"The Opta Ring is the soul of the application - a 3D glassmorphism torus that responds to user interaction. It breathes, wakes, and responds to user presence."
"The ring waking up represents Opta becoming attentive - this should feel like the AI acknowledging the user. Ring wakes from darkness, not just fades in."
"Surrounded by glass depth, neon energy trails, and particle systems that make users feel excitement and energy."

Material System: Obsidian Glassmorphism

Premium obsidian glass (NOT transparent glass - opaque volcanic stone). Dark, reflective, high-contrast appearance combining Sci-Fi HUD + Apple Refinement + Linear Sophistication.

Ring States & Behaviors

0% Energy DORMANT

Patient, waiting, observing. Plasma churns slowly like a lava lamp.

  • Color #1A0D28
  • Tilt 15° away from camera
  • Fresnel Power 8.0 (tight rim)
  • Rim Intensity 0.15 (barely visible)
  • Flow Speed 0.015
  • Y-Spin 0.1 rad/sec
0% → 50% WAKING

Bouncy, energetic spring animation. Ring rotates to face camera.

  • Duration 800ms
  • Spring stiffness: 150, damping: 20
  • Tilt Change 15° → 0° (faces camera)
  • Triggers Mouse enter, typing, click
  • Feel Bouncy overshoot then settle
50% Energy ACTIVE

Alive, responsive, engaged. Internal plasma swirls visibly.

  • Color #A855F7
  • Tilt 0° (facing camera)
  • Fresnel Power 1.5 (wide rim)
  • Rim Intensity 2.5 (bright glow)
  • Y-Spin 0.3 rad/sec
  • Timeout 3sec → dormant
Celebration EXPLODING

Multi-element effect: scale, blaze, shockwave, bloom, particles.

  • Duration 800ms total
  • Core Color #FFFFFF (white-hot)
  • Scale 1.0 → 1.15x
  • Particles 200-300 burst radially
  • Shockwave 1.0 → 3.0 radius
  • Camera Shake ±0.02 units, 50-100ms

Design Requirements Checklist

  • ✓ Look Premium — Obsidian glass with fresnel rim lighting, zero cheap plastic
  • ✓ Feel Alive — Plasma swirls inside, bob animation, responds to user presence
  • ✓ Transition Smoothly — Spring physics for all state changes (800ms wake-up)
  • ✓ Tell Stories — Energy level visible via glow intensity, color, plasma motion
  • ✓ Celebrate — Explosion with 200-300 particles, shockwave, bloom, camera shake
  • ✓ Integrate with World — Atmospheric fog breathes with ring state
  • ✓ Perform Well — 60fps minimum, <50MB GPU, adaptive quality scaling
  • ✓ Stay Accessible — Reduced motion support, multiple quality levels
  • ✓ Match References — Visual parity with 0%Opta.png and 50%Opta.png
  • ✓ Represent Purpose — Dormancy = AI patient, awakening = AI engaging
